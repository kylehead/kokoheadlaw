Koko Head

Koko Head is an Arizona native who married a Jacksonville girl in 1981. He and his wife Donna have made Jacksonville/St. Johns their home since 1988 after Koko's active duty service as a U.S. Marine Corps JAG officer. He graduated from Brigham Young University with an English degree and received his law degree from Drake University in Des Moines, Iowa in 1984. He was admitted to the Florida Bar in 1985. He is admitted to all Florida courts, the U.S. Middle District of Florida and the U.S. Eleventh Circuit. He received his Florida Real Estate license in 2019 and is a RealtorⓇ representing both sellers and buyers. He is uniquely qualified to provide exceptional services as both a real estate attorney and now a RealtorⓇ with over 34 years of experience.
