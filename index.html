The Law Office of Koko Head, P.A. (est. 1996) is a boutique business, real estate and civil litigation law firm principally serving the Northeast Florida community, but handling cases throughout the state of Florida in both state and federal courts. We represent both plaintiffs and defendants in a wide variety of business, real estate and commercial litigation matters, which include business formations, commercial disputes, contract issues, construction lien protection, construction defect, title defect removal, probate, and estate planning. We are committed to "problem solving" and a "hands on" approach. Your expectations and goals as the client are paramount and the "legal process" is discussed, planned and executed to accomplish those goals. The result is key, not the process. We look forward to assisting you with your legal needs.
